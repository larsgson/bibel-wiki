Part One — Scenes from the Old Testament

• Prologue: In the beginning
    • Genesis 1:1–5; 1:26–31; 2:7

1. From Eden to Babel
    • The Fall — Genesis 3:1–24
    • The Flood — Genesis 6:5–8; 7:17–24; 9:8–13
    • Tower of Babel — Genesis 11:1–9

2. Abraham, Isaac and Jacob
    • Call of Abraham — Genesis 12:1–3
    • God’s covenant — Genesis 15:5–6
    • Sacrifice of Isaac — Genesis 22:1–14
    • Jacob’s new name (Israel) — Genesis 32:24–28

3. Joseph in Egypt
    • Joseph sold — Genesis 37:23–28
    • Joseph interprets dreams — Genesis 41:14–16
    • Joseph forgives his brothers — Genesis 50:19–21

4. Moses and the Exodus
    • Burning bush — Exodus 3:1–10
    • Passover — Exodus 12:1–14
    • Crossing the Red Sea — Exodus 14:21–31
    • Ten Commandments — Exodus 20:1–17

5. Israel in the wilderness
    • Manna from heaven — Exodus 16:4–5, 14–15
    • Water from the rock — Exodus 17:5–6
    • The Shema — Deuteronomy 6:4–9

6. The Promised Land
    • Fall of Jericho — Joshua 6:15–20

7. Samson and Delilah
    • Samson and Delilah — Judges 16:15–21, 28–30

8. The story of Ruth
    • Ruth’s loyalty — Book of Ruth 1:16–17
    • Boaz redeems Ruth — Ruth 4:9–10

9. Samuel, Saul and David
    • Samuel’s call — 1 Samuel 3:4–10
    • David and Goliath — 1 Samuel 17:45–50
    • God’s covenant with David — 2 Samuel 7:12–16

10. Solomon the sage
    • Solomon asks for wisdom — 1 Kings 3:9–12
    • Dedication of the Temple — 1 Kings 8:27–30

11. Elijah the prophet
    • Elijah on Mount Carmel — 1 Kings 18:36–39
    • Still small voice — 1 Kings 19:11–13

12. The story of Jonah
    • Jonah and the great fish — Book of Jonah 1:15–17
    • Nineveh repents — Jonah 3:4–10

13. Exile and return
    • Fall of Jerusalem — 2 Kings 25:8–11
    • Decree of Cyrus — Book of Ezra 1:1–3

• Epilogue: A prophecy
    • The suffering servant — Book of Isaiah 53:3–7
    • The promised ruler — Isaiah 9:6–7

Part Two — Scenes from the New Testament

• Prologue: In the beginning
    • The Word — Gospel of John 1:1–5, 14

14. Jesus is born
    • Birth of Jesus — Gospel of Luke 2:1–14

15. The healer
    • Healing a leper — Gospel of Mark 1:40–42
    • Healing the paralytic — Mark 2:1–12

16. The teacher
    • Beatitudes — Gospel of Matthew 5:1–12
    • Greatest commandment — Matthew 22:37–39
    • The lost son — Gospel of Luke 15:11–24

17. The sacrifice
    • Last Supper — Matthew 26:26–28
    • Crucifixion — Matthew 27:45–50
    • Resurrection — Matthew 28:5–10

18. Acts of the apostles
    • Pentecost — Acts of the Apostles 2:1–4
    • Conversion of Paul — Acts 9:3–6

• Epilogue: A vision
    • New Heaven and New Earth — Book of Revelation 21:1–4; 22:1–5
